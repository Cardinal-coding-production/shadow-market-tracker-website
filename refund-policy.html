Refund Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. 14-Day Free Trial

All new users receive a 14-day free trial period to evaluate Shadow Market Tracker:

  • Full access to all premium features during trial
  • No charges during the trial period
  • Cancel anytime during trial with no obligations
  • Automatic conversion to paid plan after trial ends

2. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid subscriptions:

  • Full Refund: 100% refund if cancelled within 30 days of first payment
  • No Questions Asked: Simple cancellation process
  • Processing Time: Refunds processed within 5-7 business days
  • Original Payment Method: Refunds issued to original payment method

3. Pro-Rated Refunds

For subscriptions cancelled after 30 days:

  • Monthly Plans: Pro-rated refund for unused portion of current month
  • Annual Plans: Pro-rated refund for unused months
  • Calculation: Based on remaining subscription period
  • Minimum Usage: Must have used service for at least 7 days

4. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are available under the following conditions:

  • Technical issues preventing normal use of the service
  • Service not meeting advertised functionality
  • Billing errors or unauthorized charges
  • Dissatisfaction with service quality

5. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Custom development or integration work
  • Third-party API costs already incurred
  • Data export or migration services
  • Subscriptions used for more than 90 days

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Email Us: Send request to support@stm.business
  2. Include Information: Account email, subscription details, reason for refund
  3. Response Time: We respond within 24 hours
  4. Processing: Approved refunds processed within 5-7 business days

7. Cancellation Process

To cancel your subscription:

  • Account Settings: Cancel directly from your account dashboard
  • Email Request: Send cancellation request to our support team
  • Immediate Effect: Cancellation takes effect at end of current billing period
  • Data Retention: Account data retained for 30 days after cancellation

8. Payment Processor Policies

Refunds are processed through our payment partner:

  • Razorpay: 3-7 business days for all payment methods including cards, UPI, net banking, and wallets

9. Dispute Resolution

If you're not satisfied with our refund decision:

  • Contact our customer service team for review
  • Escalate to management if needed
  • We aim to resolve all disputes fairly and promptly

10. Changes to Refund Policy

We may update this refund policy periodically. Changes will be communicated via:

  • Email notification to active subscribers
  • Website announcement
  • In-app notification

11. Contact Information

For refund requests or questions:

  • Email: support@stm.business
  • Subject Line: "Refund Request - [Your Account Email]"
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours
  • Business Hours: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM IST
